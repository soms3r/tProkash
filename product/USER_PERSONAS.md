# User Personas — v0.2

---

## 1. Author

**Profile:** A Bangladeshi author looking for publishers, tracking their own books, and researching the market.

**Goals:**
- Find publishers that accept manuscripts in their genre/language
- View publisher submission guidelines and contact info
- See which publishers have published similar books
- Build a public author profile with their bibliography

**Pain points:**
- No centralized directory of Bangladeshi publishers exists
- Hard to know which publishers are actively accepting submissions
- Publisher websites are often outdated or incomplete

**v0.2 relevance:** Can browse publishers and their profiles. Can see their own author profile if data is loaded. Cannot submit manuscripts yet.

---

## 2. Publisher

**Profile:** A publishing house owner, editor, or marketing manager in Bangladesh.

**Goals:**
- Ensure their publishing house is accurately represented in the directory
- Discover other publishers for co-publishing or distribution partnerships
- Browse books in their categories to understand the market
- Access open data for industry analysis

**Pain points:**
- Their own website may not be discoverable
- No easy way to see the full landscape of Bangladeshi publishing
- Want to verify data accuracy about their own organization

**v0.2 relevance:** Can view their publisher profile and see how they appear to the public. Cannot edit their own listing yet.

---

## 3. Reader

**Profile:** A general reader interested in Bengali literature and Bangladeshi books.

**Goals:**
- Discover new books and authors
- Find where to buy books
- Look up book details (author, publisher, ISBN, description)
- Build a mental map of Bangladeshi publishing

**Pain points:**
- No single source to discover Bangladeshi books
- Limited discoverability of Bengali literature
- Hard to find book metadata (ISBN, author details)

**v0.2 relevance:** Can search and browse books, authors, and publishers. Can download datasets for personal use.

---

## 4. Researcher

**Profile:** An academic, journalist, or industry analyst researching the Bangladeshi publishing ecosystem.

**Goals:**
- Access structured, verified data about the publishing industry
- Analyze publisher distribution, book output, language trends
- Download datasets for offline analysis
- Cite verified data in publications

**Pain points:**
- Publishing industry data is fragmented or non-existent
- No reliable source for statistical analysis
- Data needs to be verifiable and citable

**v0.2 relevance:** Can download open datasets. Can browse all entities with verification status. API provides machine-readable access.

---

## 5. Bookseller

**Profile:** A bookstore owner or manager in Bangladesh sourcing books from publishers and distributors.

**Goals:**
- Find publishers and their contact information
- Discover which publishers produce books in their sales categories
- Check publisher status (active/defunct) before placing orders
- Access publisher catalogs

**Pain points:**
- Time-consuming to maintain publisher contact lists
- Hard to verify if a publisher is still active
- Limited visibility into the publisher landscape

**v0.2 relevance:** Can search publisher directory with status filters. Can view publisher profiles for contact details.

---

## 6. Administrator

**Profile:** A tProkash project maintainer responsible for data quality and platform operations.

**Goals:**
- Monitor data completeness and verification coverage
- Ensure all entities follow the defined schema and standards
- Oversee dataset releases
- Track platform usage and growth

**Pain points:**
- Need visibility into data quality metrics
- Manual verification is time-consuming
- Need to coordinate data contributions

**v0.2 relevance:** Can verify data completeness through the public profiles. Can publish datasets. Uses the API for data validation scripts.
